The Readium Foundation and EDRLab are organizing the Digital Publishing Summit 2022 in Madrid (at the Casa del Lector), on June 3. This event will focus on technical topics and will highlight the achievements of the Readium Community. 

Also, we will held a *Readium Developer Meeting* in the same Casa del Lector on May 31, during the afternoon, as a 3 hours session, with a Zoom connection for those who are remote. If you are interested joining this free technical session, please contact us asap, as we will be able to host 15 developers only.

More about the DPS and the associated Readmagine event on <a href="https://www.edrlab.org/2022/03/16/save-the-date-digital-publishing-summit-2022-madrid-june-3/">the EDRLab website</a>. 

Online booking will soon be open, and Sponsoring packages will soon be available.
